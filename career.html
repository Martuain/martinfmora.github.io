About

The practitioner
perspective

I'm Martin Fernando Mora — a structural strategist working with fintech, data platform and Web3 companies when growth has stalled and the root cause isn't the product.

My career has been spent inside the problems I now help others solve. Twelve years in operations, strategy and platform roles across Latin America, Europe and beyond — in environments where structural decisions compound: payments infrastructure, regulatory-adjacent platforms, institutional data services, and Web3 at the intersection with traditional finance.

"I work on the layer that most consultants don't touch — because it's not visible until it's already the constraint."

What I bring to engagements isn't a methodology applied from the outside. It's the pattern recognition of someone who has lived the structural tensions that emerge when a platform grows faster than its governance, or when a revenue model stops matching the value being created.

G.E.A.R. is not a consulting framework designed in theory. It's a diagnostic model built backwards from the structural problems I've seen compound — and the interventions I've watched work.
