I'm Martin Fernando Mora — a Senior Technical Product Manager and strategic advisor specializing in fintech, blockchain and data platform transformation. Based in Madrid, originally from Buenos Aires.

My career spans 18 years of end-to-end product delivery across some of the most complex, regulated technology environments: from leading Citibank's digital transformation across 12 LATAM countries, to delivering wholesale CBDC infrastructure for the European Central Bank, to advising data governance SaaS companies on structural market repositioning.

"I work where product strategy, technical architecture and market positioning intersect — because that's where the real constraints live."

What I bring to every engagement isn't just methodology — it's pattern recognition accumulated across payment systems, tokenization, enterprise SaaS, cloud migration and regulated environments across three continents. I've shipped products handling $2.5B in annual transaction volume and platforms operating at 99.95% SLA.

In parallel with product management, I consult independently on structural challenges: GTM strategy, platform positioning, data governance architecture and Web3 advisory — the work reflected in the Strategic Work section of this site.
