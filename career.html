I'm Martin Fernando Mora — a Senior Technical Product Manager and strategic advisor specializing in fintech, blockchain and data platform transformation. Based in Madrid, originally from Buenos Aires.

My career spans 18 years of end-to-end product delivery across some of the most complex, regulated environments: Citibank's digital transformation across 12 LATAM countries, wholesale CBDC infrastructure for the European Central Bank, and data governance SaaS advisory for companies entering the European market.

"I work where product strategy, technical architecture and market positioning intersect — because that's where the real constraints live."

In parallel with product management roles, I consult independently on structural challenges: GTM strategy, platform positioning, data governance and Web3 advisory — the work reflected throughout this site.
