Professional Trajectory

From delivery execution to product leadership to structural advisory in regulated markets

My career has been shaped by a consistent pattern: identifying where strategic intent fails to translate into operational reality — and building the governance, product frameworks, and delivery models to close that gap.

I began in delivery execution, learning how systems are built and scaled under operational constraints. I moved into product leadership, designing roadmaps that balanced innovation with compliance and market positioning. Now, I operate as a strategic advisor, embedding structural clarity into organizations navigating regulated expansion, scaling complexity, or product misalignment.

This trajectory reflects not career progression alone, but increasing structural responsibility — from executing within systems to designing the systems themselves.
