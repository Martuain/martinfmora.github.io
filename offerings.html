Common Questions

What to expect

Do you work with early-stage startups?
Structural misalignment tends to become visible at Series A and beyond — when growth pressure exposes the gaps in early decisions. Pre-seed and seed engagements are possible but rare.
How much time does leadership need to commit?
A Focused Intervention typically requires 2–4 hours per week from 1–2 senior leaders. This is structural work — it can't happen without leadership engagement.
Do you work across geographies?
Yes. Engagements have spanned Latin America, Europe and Southeast Asia. Work is conducted primarily remotely with in-person sessions for key milestones where possible.
What sectors do you focus on?
Fintech, B2B SaaS, data platforms, and Web3 infrastructure. The common thread is complex, regulated environments where structural decisions compound over time.
How do engagements start?
Every engagement begins with a no-commitment discovery call. If there's a structural fit, we scope a diagnostic. The diagnostic findings determine whether to proceed to an intervention.
Do you take equity or advisory shares?
Occasionally, for the right stage and alignment. The default is fee-based engagement. This is discussed openly during scoping if relevant.
